I am a professor at Cornell University. My research focuses on issues at the intereface of networks and information with and emphasis on the social and information networks that underpin the Web and other on-line media. My work has been supported by an NSF Career Award, an ONR Young Investigator Award, a Mac Arthur Foundation Fellowship, a Packard Foundation Fellowship, a Simons Investigator Award, a Simon Foundation Fellowship, and grants from Facebook, Google, Yahoo!, the ARO, and the NSF.
